About our gym

Dunedin Kickboxing is proud to announce they are teaming up with Dunedin Jiu Jistu team - The Submission Lab.

Kickboxing and Muay Thai in Dunedin is slowly growing and the DKB team are motivated to grow the sport more within the community.

Open door policy

Everybody is welcome into our gym, even if you train at another gym.

No contract

We don't lock you into a contract, you can pay as you go.

Trained champs

Our Instructors are former South Island and New Zealand champions.

Multiple classes

We schedule multiple classes at various times in the week.
